About Me

Hi! I'm Susan Merriam!
I am a visual data journalist at Columbia University’s Graduate School of Journalism and a co-founder of the Center for American Politics and Design (CAPD), a research group investigating the graphic vernacular of American politics.

Previously, I worked as a strategist and designer at the branding agency, Graj+Gustavsen on projects for commercial clients including Kohl’s, 7-Eleven, Easy~Spirit, and Men’s Wearhouse.
