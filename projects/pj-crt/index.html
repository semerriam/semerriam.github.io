[Indoctri]Nation From academia to the minds of children, how Fox News redefined critical race theory

2021


In October 2021, Shelby Jouppi, Patricia Martinez-Sastre and I worked on a data project analyzing major network coverage of "critical race theory" in comparison to the timing of lawmakers introducing legislation, "anti-CRT" protests, and Chris Rufo appearances on Fox News.

We used media coverage data gathered from the Internet TV News Archive via the GDELT Project, ACLED's events data, and legislation data from trackers by the Brookings Institution and Chris Rufo.

The ultimate goal is to make the timeline interactive by using d3 and scrollytelling to walk a reader through the timeline. Below are the static versions of what it will hopefully look like.
