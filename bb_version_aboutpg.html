About

I am a graphics data journalist at Columbia University’s Graduate School of Journalism. I co-founded the Center for American Politics and Design (CAPD), a research group investigating the graphic vernacular of American politics and occasionally speak on related subjects. Previously, I worked as a strategist and designer at the branding agency, Graj+Gustavsen, on projects for commercial clients including Kohl’s, 7-Eleven, Men’s Wearhouse, and EasySpirit.
